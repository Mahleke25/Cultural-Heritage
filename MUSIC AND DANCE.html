BASOTHO CULTURAL HERITAGE

MUSIC AND DANCE

MOKHIBO

Its form of dance backed by music for Basotho performed by Women and girls by kneeling in ordered rows and moving of shoulders to the rhythm of songs.

LITOLOBONYANA

Its a form of dance and music for Basotho ,performed by girls and women wearing maize and flour bags-made
dresses and moving of their waists up and down similar to the modern western in trend dance refered to as "Twerking".

NDLAMU

Its a form of dance backed by music for Basotho performed by mainly boys and men, rapid stomping of feet and the audience chanting songs of it.

MOHOBELO

Its a form of dance backed by music performed by Men and Boys,not as rapid as "Ndlamu" but also involves stomping of feet but softly & slow with hands holding up sticks.
