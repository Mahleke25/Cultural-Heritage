THETHANA

This is what we refer to as again, in our rich and marvelous sesotho language "Thethana", which upon translating to English is what you can call young girl's youth-wear, dedicated to: like i have already mentioned; young girls who at that age are still keen on play activities, designed to not wear them down hence the loose yet closely packed tanticle-like strings all around their waists and a bra made of animal skin(sheep or goat's perhaps).

MOSE-OA-KHOMO

On the image below is what we refer to as "Mose oa khomo" in our rich and marvelous sesotho language, which upon translating to a universal language(English),is what you can call a dress made out of a cow'skin. this beautiful elegant dress was designed to be worn by more mature as well as the elderly Basotho women for its decency feature which covers effectively sensitive areas.

SEFATLA

This is our cultural shoe,made of cow skin, handwashed to make it soft and shapeable.this shoe is rumoured to have been very warm during those olden days,heck they are probarbly still as warm.
